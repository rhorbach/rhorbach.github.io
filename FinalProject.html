Flappy Moji (FP)

On this page you can observe the progress me and my partner were able to achieve on our JavaScript based game.
In "Flappy Moji" you get to chose from one of our previously made bitmojis and control it for as long as possible.
You get an unlimited access to the gameplay, with an only way to lose by getting a -5 score, which considering the application of positive scoring is pretty difficult to do.

We consistently relied on utilizing the recently learned techniques like arrays, loops, conditionals to implement some basic mechanics. And for the most part we succeeded.
